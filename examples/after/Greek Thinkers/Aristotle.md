# Aristotle

384-322 BCE. [[Plato|Plato]]'s student for twenty years at the Academy.

Breaks with [[Plato|Plato]] on [[Forms|Forms]] — Aristotle's substances are individual, not abstract universals. The Third Man Argument ([[Metaphysics|Metaphysics]] A.9) is the famous critique of Platonic [[Forms|Forms]].

Ethics, politics, biology, logic — Aristotle systematizes almost everything. [[Plato|Plato]] stays poetic.
