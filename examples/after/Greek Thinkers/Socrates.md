# Socrates

c. 470-399 BCE. Executed by Athens for impiety and corrupting the youth. Wrote nothing. Everything we know comes from [[Plato|Plato]], Xenophon, Aristophanes.

The 'Socratic Problem': which views in the dialogues are the historical Socrates's and which are [[Plato|Plato]]'s? Almost certainly unsolvable.
