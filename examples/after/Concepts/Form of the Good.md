# Form of the Good

The highest Form. 'Beyond being' (epekeina tes ousias). Cause of both the being and the knowability of other [[Forms|Forms]].

The sun is its image. Disputed whether the Good is itself a Form or something beyond [[Forms|Forms]]. Plotinus takes it beyond; Anglo readers usually keep it in the Form-hierarchy.
