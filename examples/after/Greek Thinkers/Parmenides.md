# Parmenides

c. 515-? BCE. Eleatic. The deep background for [[Plato|Plato]]'s [[Metaphysics|metaphysics]]: 'Being is, not-being is not.'

[[Plato|Plato]]'s dialogue Parmenides stages a conversation between young [[Socrates|Socrates]] and old Parmenides. It contains the Third Man Argument in Platonic form — [[Plato|Plato]] knew the problem with his own theory.
