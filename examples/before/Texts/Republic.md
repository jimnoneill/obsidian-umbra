# Republic

Plato's longest and most ambitious dialogue. Structured around the question: what is justice, and is the just person happier?

Contains the cave (VII), the sun analogy (VI), the divided line (VI), and the myth of Er (X). Also the city-soul analogy that structures the whole argument.

Reeve's Hackett translation is standard for undergrad use. Bloom's translation is tendentious but interesting.
