# The Good as Object of Attention

> Source: [[03-25-2024]] · 2024-03-25

Murdoch's Platonism is wonderful — the Good as an object of *attention* rather than a premise in an argument. She writes: "The task of attention goes on all the time and at apparently empty and everyday moments we are 're-fastening' our vision." That's the cave's moral.
