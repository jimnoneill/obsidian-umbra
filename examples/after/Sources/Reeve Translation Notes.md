# Reeve Translation Notes

C.D.C. Reeve's Hackett translation of [[Republic|Republic]] is the standard undergrad text. Competent, literal, footnoted.

Compare with Bloom's (Basic Books) — more interpretive, good for Straussian readings. Grube/Reeve revised is the edited classic.

Reeve's introduction is underrated — the discussion of the [[Divided Line|divided line]] is particularly clear.
