# Anamnesis (Recollection)

Plato's doctrine that learning is remembering. Defended in the Meno and Phaedo.

Commits Plato to: (1) the soul existing before birth, (2) transcendent objects of knowledge, (3) the possibility of inquiry (solving Meno's paradox).

Relation to the cave: both describe the acquisition of knowledge as a *recovery* rather than a first-time discovery.
