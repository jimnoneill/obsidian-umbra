# Anamnesis (Recollection)

[[Plato|Plato]]'s doctrine that learning is remembering. Defended in the [[Meno|Meno]] and [[Phaedo|Phaedo]].

Commits [[Plato|Plato]] to: (1) the soul existing before birth, (2) transcendent objects of knowledge, (3) the possibility of inquiry (solving [[Meno|Meno]]'s paradox).

Relation to the cave: both describe the acquisition of knowledge as a *recovery* rather than a first-time discovery.
