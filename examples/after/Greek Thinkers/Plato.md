# Plato

c. 428-347 BCE. Student of [[Socrates|Socrates]], teacher of [[Aristotle|Aristotle]]. Founded the Academy.

Writes dialogues, not treatises. Never speaks in his own voice (except arguably in the disputed Seventh Letter).

Core commitments: [[Forms|Forms]], the immortality of the soul, the primacy of the Good, the educational mission of [[Philosophy|philosophy]].
