# Cave as Narrative of Sensory vs Mathematical Knowledge

> First entry: [[01-15-2024]] · 2024-01-15

Thought experiment: what if the cave is literally about sensory perception vs. mathematical knowledge? The shadows are sense-data. The puppets are physical objects. The sun is the [[Form of the Good|Form of the Good]]. Then the ascent tracks: aisthesis → doxa → dianoia → noesis. The [[Divided Line|divided line]] literally fits inside the cave. If true: the cave is a *narrative rendering* of the [[Divided Line|divided line]], not a separate argument.
