# Metaphysics

The study of what there is and how it is. [[Plato|Plato]]'s metaphysics is the [[Forms|theory of Forms]]: abstract, eternal, unchanging entities of which ordinary particulars are imperfect copies.

The [[Form of the Good|Form of the Good]] is the highest — not a Form among [[Forms|Forms]] but the ground of all [[Forms|Forms]]. Epekeina tes ousias (beyond being).
