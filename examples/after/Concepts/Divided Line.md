# Divided Line

[[Republic|Republic]] VI, 509d-511e. A geometric image of the degrees of reality and knowledge.

Four segments: (1) eikasia — images, shadows, reflections, (2) pistis — ordinary objects of sense, (3) dianoia — mathematical reasoning, (4) noesis — knowledge of [[Forms|Forms]].

The cave narratively stages the divided line.
