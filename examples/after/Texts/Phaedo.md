# Phaedo

Set on the day of [[Socrates|Socrates]]'s execution. Contains four arguments for the immortality of the soul, plus the recollection argument that matters for the cave.

If learning is recollection ([[Anamnesis|anamnesis]]), then we must have known before birth. This commits [[Plato|Plato]] to pre-existence of the soul AND transcendent objects of knowledge ([[Forms|Forms]]).
