# Epistemology

The study of knowledge. Central question: what are the conditions for knowing something, rather than merely believing it?

[[Plato|Plato]]'s answer (in [[Theaetetus|Theaetetus]]): not settled. Knowledge-as-perception fails; knowledge-as-true-belief fails; knowledge-as-true-belief-plus-logos is troubled by the Gettier-adjacent problem.

Contemporary epistemology splits into internalist and externalist camps. [[Plato|Plato]] is an internalist, mostly.
