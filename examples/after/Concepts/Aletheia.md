# Aletheia

Greek for truth. Heidegger etymologizes it as 'un-concealment' (a-lethe-ia). Heidegger argues [[Plato|Plato]] begins the reduction of truth-as-disclosure to truth-as-correctness.

Most scholars find Heidegger's reading forced, but the attention to how [[Plato|Plato]] treats truth is valuable.
