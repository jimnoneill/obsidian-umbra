# Annas — Platonic Ethics, Old and New

Julia Annas's 1999 book revisiting [[Plato|Plato]]'s ethics in light of virtue-ethics revival.

Key move: understanding the Good is not propositional — it's formative. You can't 'know' the Good without becoming different.
