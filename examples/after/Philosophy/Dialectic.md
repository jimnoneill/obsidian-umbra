# Dialectic

[[Plato|Plato]]'s preferred method of philosophical inquiry. In [[Republic|Republic]], dialectic is *downward* reasoning from hypotheses to an unhypothetical first principle (the Good).

Not debate. Not Socratic elenchus exactly. More like guided ascent toward higher principles.
