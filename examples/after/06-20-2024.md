- One on [[Plato|Plato]]'s criticism of poets in [[Republic|Republic]] X. Argues the cave is a kind of "philosophical poem" that criticizes poetry while deploying it.
- One on [[Plato|Plato]] and Buddhism. The cave's prisoner bears real resemblance to samsara. Parallel, not influence.
